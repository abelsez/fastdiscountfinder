Phase I: The 10-Second Execution Set

Use these shortcuts to "buy" time for the harder Tier-3 questions later.

1. QUANTITATIVE (Parity Check) Q: What is $124 \times 126$?
10-Second Rule: Look at the last digits ($4 \times 6 = 24$). The answer must end in 4. In 2026 MCQs, usually only one option ends in 4. Don't multiply the whole thing.
2. LOGICAL (Syllogism Strike) Q: All Pens are Boxes. Some Boxes are Red. Conclusion: Some Pens are Red.
10-Second Rule: The link between "Pens" and "Red" is broken by the "Some." If the middle term (Boxes) isn't "All" in the second statement, the conclusion is Invalid. Strike it immediately.
3. ACCOUNTING (Ratio Pulse) Q: If you pay a $1,000$ liability with $1,000$ cash, and your current ratio was $2.0$.
10-Second Rule: If Ratio $> 1.0$, reducing both sides equally increases the ratio. Don't calculate. Just pick "Increase."
