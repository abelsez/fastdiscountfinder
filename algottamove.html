📧 Allan’s Email Fix Guide

Follow slowly — nothing will break

First — What Happened (Simple Explanation)

You did nothing wrong. This happens to many people.
1. Your domain mindellrealtor.com was bought on GoDaddy
2. GoDaddy suggested setting up email → this created a Microsoft email account
3. Later, Google Workspace was also added
4. Now you have two email systems for the same address
That’s why you are paying twice and things feel confusing.
We are going to simplify everything so:
  • One email system (Google)
  • Same email address
  • Still use Outlook

Step 1: Check GoDaddy (Do NOT change anything yet)

1. Tap:
GoDaddy Login
2. Tap Sign In
3. Enter your email + password
4. After login, find My Products
5. Look for anything called:
Microsoft 365 or Email
Just confirm it exists — do NOT delete it yet.

Step 2: Check Google (Make sure email exists)

1. Tap:
Google Admin
2. Sign in using:
allan@mindellrealtor.com
3. If you can log in → good ✅
If login fails, stop here and fix password first.

Step 3: Change Where Email Goes (Important)

1. Go back to GoDaddy
2. Find:
mindellrealtor.com
3. Tap DNS or Manage DNS
4. Scroll to MX Records
MX records control where your email is delivered.
Priority 1 ASPMX.L.GOOGLE.COM Priority 5 ALT1.ASPMX.L.GOOGLE.COM Priority 5 ALT2.ASPMX.L.GOOGLE.COM Priority 10 ALT3.ASPMX.L.GOOGLE.COM Priority 10 ALT4.ASPMX.L.GOOGLE.COM
5. Delete old MX records (Microsoft ones)
6. Add the Google ones above exactly
Take your time. This is the most important step.

Step 4: Wait

Wait 30–60 minutes
Send a test email to yourself
Check if it arrives

Step 5: Fix Outlook

1. Open Outlook
2. Remove the old account
3. Add account again
4. Sign in with your email
Outlook will now connect to Google.

Final Step (Only After Everything Works)

1. Go back to GoDaddy
2. Find Microsoft Email subscription
3. Cancel it
Only cancel after confirming email works perfectly.
