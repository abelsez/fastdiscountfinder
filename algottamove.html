📧 Simple Email Fix Guide

Follow slowly — nothing will break

Step 1: Open GoDaddy

1. Tap this link:
GoDaddy Login
2. Tap Sign In (top right)
3. Enter your email + password
4. After login, look for "My Products"
If you don’t see "My Products", scroll or tap your profile icon.
5. Find anything that says:
Microsoft 365 or Email
Do NOT delete anything yet.

Step 2: Open Google Admin

1. Tap:
Google Admin
2. Sign in using:
allan@mindellrealtor.com
3. If login works, you’re good ✅
If password fails, stop here and recover account first.

Step 3: Change Email Delivery (Important)

1. Go back to GoDaddy
2. Find your domain:
mindellrealtor.com
3. Tap DNS or Manage DNS
4. Scroll down to MX Records
MX = where your email goes. We are pointing it to Google.
Priority 1 ASPMX.L.GOOGLE.COM Priority 5 ALT1.ASPMX.L.GOOGLE.COM Priority 5 ALT2.ASPMX.L.GOOGLE.COM Priority 10 ALT3.ASPMX.L.GOOGLE.COM Priority 10 ALT4.ASPMX.L.GOOGLE.COM
5. Delete old MX records (usually Microsoft ones)
6. Add the Google ones above EXACTLY
Take your time. One mistake = email not working.

Step 4: Wait

Wait 30–60 minutes
Then send a test email to yourself
Check if it arrives in Gmail

Step 5: Fix Outlook

1. Open Outlook on your computer
2. Remove the old account
3. Add account again
4. Login using your email
Outlook will now connect to Google automatically.

Final Step (Only After Everything Works)

1. Go back to GoDaddy
2. Find Microsoft Email subscription
3. Cancel it
ONLY cancel after confirming email works.
