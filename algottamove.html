📧 Allan’s Email Fix Guide

Follow step by step — nothing will break

First — What Happened

You didn’t do anything wrong — this is very common.
1. Your domain mindellrealtor.com was bought on GoDaddy
2. GoDaddy set up a Microsoft email (Outlook system)
3. Later, Google Workspace was also added
4. Now you have TWO email systems for ONE address
That’s why things feel confusing and you may be paying twice.
We will fix this so:
  • ✔ Everything is stored in Google
  • ✔ You still use Outlook
  • ✔ Only ONE system remains

⚠️ Important Before Starting

Your OLD emails are currently stored in Microsoft (Outlook system).
They will NOT move automatically.
If we skip migration, old emails may disappear from Outlook.
We will safely move EVERYTHING into Google first.

Step 1: Move Old Emails into Google (Automatic)

1. Open: Google Admin
2. Log in using: allan@mindellrealtor.com
3. On the dashboard, find and click: Data → Data Migration
4. Click: Set Up Data Migration
5. Choose: Microsoft Exchange
6. Enter your email + Outlook password
7. Start migration
Google will copy ALL your old emails automatically
This may take some time — wait until emails appear in Gmail

Step 2: Confirm Emails Are in Google

Open Gmail and check:
Open Gmail
Make sure you see your old emails
Do NOT continue until you see them

Step 3: Change Where New Emails Go

1. Open: GoDaddy
2. Login → My Products
3. Find your domain: mindellrealtor.com
4. Click: DNS / Manage DNS
5. Scroll to MX Records
Priority 1 ASPMX.L.GOOGLE.COM Priority 5 ALT1.ASPMX.L.GOOGLE.COM Priority 5 ALT2.ASPMX.L.GOOGLE.COM Priority 10 ALT3.ASPMX.L.GOOGLE.COM Priority 10 ALT4.ASPMX.L.GOOGLE.COM
6. Delete ALL old MX records (Microsoft ones)
7. Add the Google ones above EXACTLY
Now NEW emails will go to Google

Step 4: Connect Outlook to Google

1. Download: Google Workspace Sync
2. Install it
3. Sign in using your email
4. Open Outlook
Outlook will now show your Google emails
Outlook = app you use Google = where emails are stored

Final Step

1. Send a test email (send + receive)
2. Confirm everything works
3. Go back to GoDaddy
4. Cancel Microsoft email subscription
You now have ONE clean system
