📧 Allan’s Step‑by‑Step Email Migration Guide

Your domain: mindellrealtor.com
Your email account: allan@mindellrealtor.com

This guide walks you through moving your email safely from Microsoft Exchange to Google Workspace, while continuing to use Microsoft Outlook exactly as you do today.

✅ 1. What Is Changing (and What Is Not)

  • Your email address does not change.
  • You will continue using Microsoft Outlook.
  • Google Workspace (Gmail) will store and deliver your email.
  • Microsoft Exchange will stop hosting email once everything is confirmed.

Think of Outlook as the steering wheel and Google as the engine powering your email.

🗺 2. How Your Email Will Work

You open Outlook on your computer
↓
Outlook connects using Google’s official sync tool
↓
Your email lives securely in Google Workspace (Gmail)
↓
Messages are sent and received over the internet

From your point of view, Outlook looks and works the same.

🧭 3. Step One – Sign in to Microsoft (Your Current Email)

This step is only to allow your existing email to be copied to Google.

  1. Go to: https://admin.microsoft.comhttps://admin.microsoft.com
  2. Sign in using your Microsoft 365 Global Admin account.
  3. Confirm that you can see your existing mailbox and email data.
You are not deleting anything here. This is read‑only access used to copy email safely.

🧭 4. Step Two – Sign in to Google Workspace

  1. Go to: https://admin.google.comhttps://admin.google.com
  2. Sign in using your Google Workspace admin account for mindellrealtor.com.
  3. Verify that your domain mindellrealtor.com is listed.
  4. Create your mailbox (if not already created).

This is where your email will live going forward.

🧭 5. Step Three – Find Your Domain Registrar

This step tells the internet where to deliver your email.

  1. If you already know your registrar (GoDaddy, Namecheap, etc.), log in there.
  2. If you are unsure, go to: https://lookup.icann.orghttps://lookup.icann.org
  3. Search for mindellrealtor.com.
  4. Note the company listed as the registrar.
  5. Log in to that registrar’s control panel.

📌 6. Step Four – Update MX Records (Email Routing)

Inside your registrar’s DNS or Email settings, find MX Records, then replace the existing ones with the following:

  • Priority 1 – ASPMX.L.GOOGLE.COM
  • Priority 5 – ALT1.ASPMX.L.GOOGLE.COM
  • Priority 5 – ALT2.ASPMX.L.GOOGLE.COM
  • Priority 10 – ALT3.ASPMX.L.GOOGLE.COM
  • Priority 10 – ALT4.ASPMX.L.GOOGLE.COM
Once saved, new email will begin flowing to Google Workspace. This can take up to an hour and usually much less.

🧭 7. Step Five – Connect Outlook to Google

  1. Install Google Workspace Sync for Outlook (GWSMO).
  2. Remove your old Exchange Outlook profile.
  3. Sign in with your Google Workspace email.
  4. Allow Outlook to sync email, calendar, and contacts.

After this step, Outlook continues as normal — just backed by Google.

✅ 8. Final Confirmation & Cleanup

  • Send and receive test emails.
  • Confirm older email is present.
  • Once satisfied, cancel the Exchange subscription.
  • Change any admin passwords you used.

🧘 Final Reassurance

Nothing about your daily email usage changes.

Same Outlook. Same email address. Lower cost. Modern backend.
