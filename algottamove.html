📧 Email Migration Guide

Domain: mindellrealtor.com
Primary Admin/User: allan@mindellrealtor.com

Goal: Reduce email costs by moving from Microsoft Exchange to Google Workspace while keeping Outlook unchanged.

✅ 1. Purpose (Plain English)

We are changing where email is hosted, not how it is used.

  • Outlook desktop stays the same
  • Email address stays the same
  • Google Workspace (Gmail) becomes the email system
  • Microsoft Exchange stops hosting mailboxes

Outlook is the steering wheel. Google Workspace is the engine.

📌 3. Domain & MX Records (Critical)

To ensure all new email goes to Google Workspace, the domain mindellrealtor.com must point its MX records to Google.

Required MX Records

These records are provided by Google and must be entered exactly as shown:

  • Priority 1 – ASPMX.L.GOOGLE.COM
  • Priority 5 – ALT1.ASPMX.L.GOOGLE.COM
  • Priority 5 – ALT2.ASPMX.L.GOOGLE.COM
  • Priority 10 – ALT3.ASPMX.L.GOOGLE.COM
  • Priority 10 – ALT4.ASPMX.L.GOOGLE.COM
Action required by Allan:
Log into the domain registrar for mindellrealtor.com, locate the MX / Mail Settings section, and replace existing MX records with the ones listed above.

Email will continue working during this change. Google becomes active once DNS updates propagate.

  • Google Workspace Sync for Outlook is installed
  • User signs in with Google Workspace account
  • Outlook reconnects to Gmail automatically

From the user’s perspective, Outlook looks and behaves the same.
