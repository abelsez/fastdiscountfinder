📧 Allan’s Email Cleanup & Migration Guide

Domain: mindellrealtor.com
Email covered: allan@mindellrealtor.com

This guide helps you clean up a common GoDaddy email situation and move to a simpler, lower‑cost setup while keeping Microsoft Outlook.

✅ 1. Why This Happened (Very Common with GoDaddy)

This is a very common situation for domain owners — you didn’t do anything wrong.

  • Your domain was registered on GoDaddy.
  • During setup, GoDaddy strongly promoted Microsoft email.
  • GoDaddy created and sold a Microsoft Exchange mailbox.
  • Later, Google Workspace was added separately.
Result: You ended up paying for two email services for the same single email address.

✅ 2. What We Are Doing

  • You keep using Microsoft Outlook.
  • Your email address stays the same.
  • Google Workspace becomes the only email system.
  • The GoDaddy Microsoft email subscription is canceled at the end.

🗺 3. How Your Email Will Work

Open Outlook on your computer
↓
Outlook connects using Google Workspace Sync
↓
Email lives in Google (Gmail)
↓
Messages go to and from the internet

Outlook is the steering wheel. Google is the engine.

🧭 4. Step One – Log in to GoDaddy

  1. Go to https://www.godaddy.comhttps://www.godaddy.com
  2. Sign in with the account that owns mindellrealtor.com.
  3. Click My Products.
  4. Look for Microsoft 365 / Email under the domain.
Do not cancel anything yet — this is just to confirm what exists.

🧭 5. Step Two – Log in to Google Workspace

  1. Go to https://admin.google.comhttps://admin.google.com
  2. Sign in as admin for mindellrealtor.com.
  3. Confirm the mailbox allan@mindellrealtor.com exists.

📌 6. Step Three – Update MX Records in GoDaddy

In GoDaddy, open DNS settings for mindellrealtor.com and update MX records to:

  • Priority 1 – ASPMX.L.GOOGLE.COM
  • Priority 5 – ALT1.ASPMX.L.GOOGLE.COM
  • Priority 5 – ALT2.ASPMX.L.GOOGLE.COM
  • Priority 10 – ALT3.ASPMX.L.GOOGLE.COM
  • Priority 10 – ALT4.ASPMX.L.GOOGLE.COM
This tells the internet to deliver all new email to Google. Changes usually take under one hour.

🧭 7. Step Four – Connect Outlook to Google

  1. Install Google Workspace Sync for Outlook.
  2. Remove the old Exchange profile.
  3. Sign in using allan@mindellrealtor.com.

✅ 8. Final Step – Cancel the GoDaddy Microsoft Email

  1. Confirm Outlook is working normally.
  2. Confirm old emails are visible.
  3. Return to GoDaddy → My Products.
  4. Cancel the Microsoft email subscription.
Only cancel after everything is confirmed working.

🧘 Final Reassurance

  • One email address
  • One email system
  • One bill instead of two
  • The same Outlook experience
